About Clue Hunt

This is a game for you to test your powers of deduction.
Use your wits to explore a dangerous warehouse and find which room the criminal is hiding in.
Navigate using your powers of perception to avoid hazardous rooms.

Rules

  1. There are 3 hazards:
    1. An alarm. Tripping the alarm will cause the criminal to escape and you to lose. (you will hear ticking when adjacent to the room with the alarm).
    2. Disorientating gas. Gas that will move you to a random space possibly one with an alarm or the criminal(you will see green gas when adjacent to the room with disorientating gas).
    3. The criminal who will flee from you on sight. (you will smell cigar smoke when adjacent to the room with the criminal).
  2. The criminal will not trip the alarm so they can be in the same room.
  3. The criminal is not affected by the disorientating gas so they can be in the same room.
  4. The alarm and disorientating gas will not be in the same room.
  5. There is only 1 room with an alarm and one room with disorientating gas
  6. Guessing an incorrect room may cause the criminal to move to another room.
  7. You have 5 guesses.
  8. You may find an an extra guess in the warehouse.
  9. An element of luck as always is involved.

Thoughts

The best approach to winning the game in my opinion is to first try and find as many of the safe rooms as you can.
My partner Holmes though prefers to test himself and try to find the criminal in as few moves as possible.
